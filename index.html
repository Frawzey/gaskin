About Gaskin Engineering

GASKIN ENGINEERING COMPANY LIMITED is incorporated in Nigeria under registration number RC 808812 was established to deliver satisfactory and exceptional Engineering services to clients in various fields, including Facilities Management, Instrumentation/Control, Electronic Security and Fire Safety Systems, Electrical/Electronic Engineering, Electrical Power Equipment, Information Technology, Logistics and Supply Chain Management, and Construction.

Our Vision

To focus on customers' needs by providing excellent innovative products and exceptional service in order to consistently add value to our clients and their respective industries.

Our Mission

  • Deliver Value for client's Investments
  • Ensure compliance to Regulators/International Safety Standards
  • Provide cost-effective, timely and quality innovative solutions

Our Approach

We have adopted Deming's Total Quality Management (TQM) culture with a focus on internal network and chain of client-supplier relationships. With the latest plant and equipment, strong financial leverage, years of experience, highly motivated human resources, and strict adherence to international safety standards, Gaskin Engineering holds its position firmly in Nigeria's engineering sector.
